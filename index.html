The Salmon Cookie

The salmon cookie is a one of a kind Pacific North West exclusive. Wild salmon caught fresh everyday from the Puget sound is filleted, cooked, and ground everyday in house before being added to our super sweet cookie dough. The combination of smoky salmon and sugar cookie makes a unique flavor only residents of Seattle Washington can experiance.
